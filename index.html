About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on preparing professionals for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments.

Through collaboration with UK universities and industry partners, IDR combines academic insight with real-world projects to train the next generation of risk professionals.

Our Model

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps designed for students and professionals to build expertise in digital and cyber risk.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing new intellectual property, AI governance frameworks and future digital risk models.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement digital risk frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2.

Who We Serve

Our community includes students, early-career professionals and experienced practitioners from sectors such as financial services, technology and critical infrastructure who are seeking to upskill in digital and cyber risk.
